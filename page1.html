Since the Paris agreement global banks have increased their support for fossil fuels,  with trillions of €$£ flowing to the coal, oil and gas industry and its new projects

Banks provide loans and credit to companies like Exxon or Shell. Importantly, they also provide the cash for new fossil fuel infrastructure projects like a tar sands pipelines or coal mines. Any company that wants to build a new fossil fuel megaproject, needs banks to back it.

But people are rising up to block the money pipeline

Disrutpting banks is going to be an increaing important tactic in the months and years to come. Until commercial banks stop funding climate destrcution or the central banks and regulators but in place the rules to stop them, they will need to deal with increasing disrutpion from the climate movement

To stop climate breakdown commercial banks need to immediately freeze any new finance for fossil fuels

Then demonstrate plans for phasing out all existing finance over the next few years. Everything else is greenwash.
Distant net-zero pledges aren't going to cut it.
