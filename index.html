We are prototyping a new framework/methodology
to accelerate how our movements colaborate and intersect to build power

Red Circles are community caucuses: small, focused and guided group conversations aimed at establishing political alignment within a specific community and establishing collective organising structures.

We are a loose group of veteran climate activists with experience of mobilising and organising around COPs. Our aim is to help create a new movement framework, focused on crowdsourcing a new political common sense.

At its core the concept that we're working on is radically simple. It is based on the assumption that in order to avoid ecological collapse and win the struggle for racial and economic justice, we're going to have to prioritise work at the intersections of our movements and with a level of coordination that we've not seen or tried before.
